Our Mission

For this website, our mission was to create an easy to use interface which highlights the issue of homelessness beyond simply the instances you can see. Although the data we use is already avaliable to the public, it is tucked away behind a veil of clutter and confusion.

Re:Thinking Homelessness will hopefully remove that veil to help enlighten those who wish to learn more about this horrible issue plaguing our country
